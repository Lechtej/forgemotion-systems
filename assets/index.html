Built from Aviation Discipline. Made for Modern Simulation.

Motion technology has existed for decades — historically reserved for airline and military training. Today, accessible components and digital engineering opened the door for smaller teams and enthusiasts. ForgeMotion Systems brings that “big aviation” mindset into platforms you can own, customize, and trust.

From CAD to Real Hardware

We design and build solutions for people and business — from affordable, customizable B2C platforms to robust setups for training and events. Every product is developed hands-on: CAD design, rapid prototyping, and manufacturing using 3D printing and CNC — then assembly, tuning, and testing.

From TEDx to Motion Platforms

At TEDx, the “Dream Simulator” story showed how a personal idea becomes engineering reality. That mindset fuels ForgeMotion Systems: build precise, reliable motion that feels authentic — for racing, flight, training, and pure passion.

Why Trust ForgeMotion Systems?

Our approach is grounded in real aviation practice: digitalizing operational processes, cockpit ergonomics, and training tooling — then translating that discipline into motion systems built for modern simulation.
