# FAQ – VAT & International Orders (ForgeMotion Systems)

**Is VAT included?**  
Tax/VAT treatment depends on the delivery country and Customer status (B2C/B2B) and is specified in the offer/contract.

**Do you ship to the UK / USA / non‑EU countries?**  
Yes. Local import taxes, duties, and fees may apply.

**Are customs duties and import taxes included in the price?**  
Generally no, unless explicitly stated in the offer/contract.
