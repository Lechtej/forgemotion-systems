FAQ

Is 6DOF only for flight sims?

No — 6DOF can be configured for both aircraft and vehicles. The goal is believable full-body cues and repeatable control feedback.

How complex is installation?

The platform is built for practical setup: modular architecture and minimal wiring (mains power + USB per module). Tell us your space and cockpit type.

Can it be adapted to my rig?

Yes. The adjustable frame and modular approach make it easier to integrate with different cockpit layouts and upgrade paths.
